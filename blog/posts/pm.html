On Product Management

How I Work

  1. I talk to my customers frequently and aim to fully understand their problems. I try to feel the pain firsthand (by replicating it if I can) whenever possible to better empathize with them.
  2. I always answer these three questions when working on features: What are you building? Who is it for? Why are we building it?
  3. Roadmapping is not only important, it's basically the job. The next quarter should be well planned and I try to always have a rough idea of 2-3 quarters out. I like to have public roadmaps that inform customers about common/big asks. Something like "Now, Next, Future" is fine for that. A public voting forum is even better so you can easily see things your customers want.
