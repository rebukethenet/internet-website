On Product Management

My Tenets

  1. Talk to your customers and understand their problems. Feel the pain firsthand (i.e. replicate) whenever possible to better empathize.
  2. You should always know: What are you building? Who is it for? Why are we building it?
  3. Have a public roadmap. Something like "Now, Next, Future" is fine. A public voting forum is even better.
    • Render.com's public roadmap and feature voting
    • GitHub's using their own project tracking
    • Slack uses Trello
